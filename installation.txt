### INSTALLATION GUIDE ###

1. UPLOAD FOLDERS "config" and "plugins" TO YOUR UASECO INSTALLATION

2. ADD <plugin>plugin.social_media.php</plugin> TO THE plugins.xml IN CONFIG FOLDER

3. RESTART YOUR Uaseco INSTALLATION

4. FOR FIRST START A DEFAULT ICON AND URL IS SET

### EDIT "config/social_media.xml TO YOUR NEEDS. YOU CAN CHECK YOUR SETTINGS WITH CHAT COMMAND "/socialreload". NO RESTART OF Uaseco IS NEEDED! SOMETIMES A MAP CHANGE WILL DO THE REST ###

Following entries should be changed:

- <height> and <width> based on the buttons you want to use

- <pos_y> in "race" and "score" mode (vertical position of your slider

- <icon> the link to your icon

- <icon_focus> the link to your focus (hover) icon (you can leave emtpy for static view)

- <url> the url to follow
